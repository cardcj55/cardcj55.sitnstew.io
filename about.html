Our Story

Comfort food, warm soups, and a cozy place to slow down.

How Sit n' Stew Started

Sit n' Stew Café was created with one simple idea: bring people together over warm, comforting bowls of soup. Whether you're stopping in for a quick lunch or relaxing with friends, our café offers a cozy atmosphere and homemade flavors.

We focus on fresh ingredients, seasonal soups, and simple foods that pair perfectly with them — warm bread, sandwiches, and small sides.

What We Believe In

Fresh Ingredients

Every soup is prepared with quality ingredients and homemade care.

Comfort & Community

Our café is a place to relax, talk, and enjoy good food together.

Seasonal Variety

Our menu rotates soups regularly so there is always something new.
