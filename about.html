About Our Café

Sit n' Stew Café was created with one goal in mind: to bring people together over warm, comforting meals.

Our soups and stews are made fresh daily using simple, high-quality ingredients and slow cooking techniques.

Whether you're stopping in for lunch or warming up on a cold day, we believe every bowl should feel like home.
